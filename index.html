Email Composer for Gmail

Send emails quickly from a Gmail sidebar panel.

What is Email Composer?

Email Composer is a Google Workspace Add-on that lets you compose and send emails directly from the Gmail sidebar. It provides a simple form with To, Subject, and Body fields — no switching tabs, no extra steps.

How to use it?

Email Composer is a Google Workspace Add-on that lets you compose and send emails directly from the Gmail sidebar. It provides a simple form with To, Subject, and Body fields — no switching tabs, no extra steps.

Permissions this add-on requests

  • .../auth/userinfo.email & .../auth/userinfo.profile — Used automatically by Google to verify your identity and display your email address within the active session.
  • .../auth/gmail.addons.execute — Used solely to render and display the Email Composer user interface inside your Gmail sidebar panel.
  • .../auth/script.send_mail — Used solely to deliver the emails you explicitly write and send using the sidebar form. No other email data is ever accessed or read.
